CTR & Website Performance

This presentation explains CTR, website performance parameters, and performance tools in a simple and professional way.

What is CTR?

CTR means Click Through Rate. It shows how many people click after seeing your content.

CTR Formula

CTR = (Number of Clicks ÷ Number of Impressions) × 100

Why CTR is Important

  • Shows if your content is attractive
  • Improves marketing success
  • Helps increase sales

Website Performance Parameters

  • Speed
  • CTR
  • Bounce Rate
  • Conversion Rate
  • Mobile Performance

What is Bounce Rate?

Bounce rate shows how many users leave your website without interacting.

Website Performance Tools

  • Google Analytics
  • PageSpeed Insights
  • GTmetrix
  • Search Console

Why We Use Performance Tools

  • Improve website speed
  • Increase visitors
  • Improve user experience
  • Increase conversions
