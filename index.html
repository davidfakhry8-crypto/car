What is Click Through Rate (CTR)?

CTR measures how many users click after seeing content.

Formula: (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100

Example: 20 clicks from 200 views = 10%

Why CTR Is Important

  • Shows user interest
  • Improves ads
  • Improves SEO
  • Increases conversion

Where CTR Is Used

  • Websites
  • Search engines
  • Social media
  • Online stores
